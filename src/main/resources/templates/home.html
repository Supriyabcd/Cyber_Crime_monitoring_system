Reporting Process

Our streamlined three-step process ensures your report is handled professionally and securely.

1. Identify

Recognize the cybersecurity incident. Document any evidence, screenshots, emails, or suspicious activities. Preserve all relevant information.

2. Report

Submit a detailed report through our secure platform. Include all evidence and provide as much context as possible for investigation.

3. Resolve

Our expert team investigates and works with authorities. You'll receive updates and guidance on protecting yourself from future threats.
